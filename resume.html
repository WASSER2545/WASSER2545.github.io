RESEARCH INTEREST
 
Yongliang Shen is a final-year Ph.D. student in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University. His research topics of interest include:
 
•
 
Cognitive Agents
 
especially on LLM-powered autonomous agents, focusing on the abilities of LLMs for human-level reasoning, planning, tool invocation, and environment interaction.
 
•
 
Knowledge Graph
 
especially on information extraction (e.g. named entity recognition, relation extraction) and knowledge-enhanced LMs.
