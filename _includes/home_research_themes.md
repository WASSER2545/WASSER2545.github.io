## Research Themes

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Cloud Database Benchmarking</h3>
    <p>I study how to evaluate cloud analytics systems with workloads that better reflect realistic data statistics, query patterns, and deployment settings.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>Workload Synthesis</h3>
    <p>I am interested in generating representative and privacy-aware workloads that make benchmarking more useful for both research and production-facing systems.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="info-card">
    <h3>AI for Data Systems</h3>
    <p>I care about how learning-based methods can support data system tuning, query optimization, and workload understanding without sacrificing rigor in evaluation.</p>
  </div>
</div>
